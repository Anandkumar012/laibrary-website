Facilities

  • Timing

    Open from 9 AM to 5 PM.

  • Books

    • Novels

      Our library offers a collection of motivational, inspirational, and fictional novels to improve reading skills and encourage positive thinking among students.

    • Competitive Exams

      We provide study materials for SSC, Banking, Railway, Police, and other competitive examinations. These books help students prepare with practice questions, previous year papers, and concept explanations.

    • School Books

      We provide NCERT and reference books for school students from different classes, helping them strengthen their academic foundation.

    • Spiritual Books

      The library also includes spiritual and moral books that promote peace, focus, and personal growth for readers of all ages.

  • Computer Courses

    We offer basic computer training for students and beginners, including MS Word, MS Excel, and fundamental computer knowledge. This course helps learners improve their digital skills and prepare for academic and competitive exams.

    • MS Word

      The MS Word training focuses on document creation and formatting. Students learn how to create letters, resumes, reports, and official documents.

    • MS Excel

      The MS Excel training helps students understand spreadsheet basics, data entry, and simple formulas for organizing data.

    • Other Basic Computer Skills

  • 20+ Study Seats

    Our library provides 20 comfortable and well-arranged study seats, ensuring a quiet and focused environment for students.

  • High-Speed WiFi

    Free high-speed WiFi is available for students to access online study materials, research content, and educational resources.

  • Comfortable Study Environment

    The library is equipped with fans, coolers, proper lighting, and charging points at each seat to ensure a comfortable study experience.
